About CUT-Guide

Campus Navigation & Information System for Chinhoyi University of Technology

Overview

CUT-Guide is a comprehensive Campus GIS Navigation System designed to help students, staff, and visitors navigate the Chinhoyi University of Technology campus efficiently.

The application leverages GPS coordinates and interactive mapping technology to provide real-time navigation, facility information, and campus exploration tools.

Key Features

  • Interactive Map: Explore the campus with real-time GPS coordinates and facility markers
  • Smart Search: Find buildings, rooms, services, and facilities instantly
  • Directions: Get turn-by-turn directions to any campus location via Google Maps
  • Building Info: View detailed information about campus buildings, floors, and rooms
  • Service Points: Locate medical centers, libraries, offices, labs, and other facilities
  • Accessibility: Filter services and buildings by accessibility features
  • User Accounts: Create accounts to save bookmarks and personalize your experience
  • Distance Calculation: Measure distances between campus locations

Getting Started

  1. Explore the Map: Start on the homepage to see all campus buildings and service points
  2. Search: Use the search bar to find specific locations or services
  3. View Details: Click on any building or service to see comprehensive information
  4. Get Directions: Click the "Get Directions" button to navigate to any location
  5. Create Account: Sign up to personalize your experience and save bookmarks
  6. Navigate Efficiently: Use the distance and route information to plan your journey
